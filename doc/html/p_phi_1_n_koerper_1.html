Die Kerninnovation

\[ \mathbf{F} = -\mathbf{F}_\text{Newton} \left(1 - \frac{(\dot{\mathbf{r}})^2}{c^2} + \frac{\mathbf{r} \cdot \ddot{\mathbf{r}}}{2c^2}\right) \]

\[ \mathbf{p}(\phi) = \frac{L}{a(1 - e^2)} \left[ e \sin \phi (1 + e \cos \phi) \hat{r} + (1 + e \cos \phi) \hat{\phi} \right] \]
